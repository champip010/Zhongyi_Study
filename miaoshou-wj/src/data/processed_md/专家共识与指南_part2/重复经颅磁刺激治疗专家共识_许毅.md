# Chinese experts consensus on repetitive transcranial tagnetic stimulation

Chinese Society of ECT& Neurostimulation

Abstract The Chinese Neuromodulation Society experts were commissoned to written a consensus and treatment recommendation for the therapeutic use of repetitive transcranial magnetic stimulation（rTMS） from evidence published up until November 2O17regarding depressionpain movement disordersstroke epilepsy tinnitusanxiety disordersobsessive-compulsive disorder schizophrenia craving/addiction and sleep disorderseach summary clinical recommendation is presented with the substantiating peer-reviewedpublished evidence supporting that recommendation.It summarizes current evidence for the safety and efficacy of the use of rTMS therapyaims to determine how to optimize rTMS protocols and techniques to give them relevance in routine clinical practice. In addition professionals carrying out rTMS protocols should undergo rigorous training to ensure the quality of the technical realizationguarantee the proper care of patientsand maximize the chances of success.
